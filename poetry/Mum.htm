In Love, Again

We know, in time things heal
The heart that is broken, mends
The paradigm, shifts
The world, changes

It’s true, in time things are forgotten
The memories, fade
The lives, diverge
The haunts, exorcised

But also, in time things grow
The wrong, rights
The frustrating, endears
The impossible, permits

Neal Tibrewala 9/19/04
