Journey

So common their differences, yet different their commonness
Two lovers blazing a trail walked many times before
The destination unknown, the journey certain
The way, perilous, fraught with Hopes, Dreams, Desires
Will they be lost? Found? Saved? Forgiven?
For each guidance found only in the other

This is a recent love angst poem.  I like it because it is layered (like an onion) and I'm not sure even I have discovered all it has to tell.
