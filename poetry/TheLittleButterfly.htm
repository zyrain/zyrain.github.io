The Little Butterfly

I want to see this and go over there
Fluttering by without even a care
I land on a flower to rest my wings
Flowers are always such beautiful things
Now back over here, and again over there
Thanks to my wings I fly through the air
And land once again so that all bugs can see
I'm the prettiest bug as there ever can be

I like the rhyme and meter in this work.  It's one of my few that needs to be read to catch the fully effect, but most people can pick up on it after a reading or two.  I intend a slight speed increase after the first line and a slow down again for the last two lines.
